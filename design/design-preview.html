Decisions in this pass, for sign-off

  • Name. Proposing "The ACA Scorecard" as the user-facing name. Your strategy file already calls the list the ACA Scorecard email list. Say the word and it stays, or give me your name.
  • Gold discipline. Landing and question screens carry exactly one gold moment each (the start CTA, the progress bar). The results screen is one long scroll and carries two: the leak pillar marker and the email capture CTA. That is a deliberate stretch of the one-per-design rule so the eye lands on the leak first and the capture second. Tell me if you want it stricter.
  • Placeholders, not final copy. The three moves, the capture copy, and the waitlist copy are my drafts from your framework file. They are placeholders until you confirm them in Phase 3 and Phase 5. The verdict and pillar reads are the audit copy you already shipped.
  • Results before capture. Results show immediately, email is optional and sits after the action plan. No gate in front of the score.
